Common Questions

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes! You can pause for up to 8 weeks without any penalties. Just manage it in your account settings.

How does delivery work?

We deliver every week on your chosen day. All meals arrive in insulated packaging to ensure freshness.

Can I change my plan?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect in your next billing cycle.

What if I don't like a meal?

We offer meal swaps instantly through your account. No questions asked if you want something different.

Do you offer gift subscriptions?

Yes! Give the gift of healthy eating. Choose any plan and duration for your loved ones.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No minimum commitment at all. Cancel your subscription anytime with no fees or penalties.
